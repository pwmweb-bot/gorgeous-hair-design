Wedding Hair FAQs

How much does wedding hair cost in Droitwich?

Bridal hair at Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich Spa starts from £75. We also offer bridal trials and tailored packages for bridal parties. Your stylist will provide a personalised quote at your consultation based on your chosen style and the number of people in your party.

Should I have a wedding hair trial?

We always recommend a bridal trial. It gives us the chance to perfect your style, try different options, and make sure your hair holds beautifully — so you walk into your wedding day feeling completely confident. We suggest booking your trial 4 to 8 weeks before the wedding.

Can you do hair for my bridesmaids and family too?

Yes — we offer full bridal party packages for bridesmaids, mothers of the bride and groom, flower girls and more. Just let us know your numbers and we'll plan timings so everyone is styled and ready in plenty of time.

How far ahead should I book?

Wedding dates fill up fast, particularly over spring and summer. We recommend booking as soon as your date is confirmed — ideally 6 to 12 months in advance — to secure your preferred stylist. Call us on 01905 799055 to check availability.

Where will my wedding hair be done?

Many brides choose to have their hair styled at our welcoming Droitwich Spa salon, while others prefer styling at their venue. Get in touch to discuss your plans and we'll talk through the best option for your day.
