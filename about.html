About Us

18 years of passion, expertise and care in Droitwich

Our Story

Gorgeous Hair Design was founded in 2006 by Kylie Kelly with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where hair care meets genuine passion. What started as a small independent salon has grown into a beloved destination for clients across Droitwich and beyond, all while maintaining the personal touch that defines us.

Over 18 years, we've developed a deep understanding of what great hair care means. It's not just about the technical skill—though we never compromise on that. It's about listening to our clients, understanding their needs, and delivering results that exceed expectations. Every appointment is an opportunity to build relationships and earn trust.

Today, we're proud to offer a comprehensive range of services under one roof: expert hair cutting and styling, vibrant colour and balayage, beautiful wedding and special occasion hair, professional aesthetics treatments, and qualified body piercing. Our team of talented stylists and specialists bring dedication, creativity, and care to every client who walks through our door.

Our Values

Excellence

We never cut corners. Every service is delivered to the highest standard, with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to quality that defines our work.

Care

Every client is our priority. We take time to understand your needs, offer honest advice, and create a welcoming space where you feel valued and heard.

Passion

We love what we do. Our team's enthusiasm for hair, beauty, and helping you look and feel your best shines through in everything we create.

We believe great hair can change how you feel about yourself. That's why we treat every appointment as an opportunity to make someone's day.

— Kylie Kelly, Founder
