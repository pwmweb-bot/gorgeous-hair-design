Piercing FAQs

How much does body piercing cost in Droitwich?

Body piercing at Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich Spa starts from £30 for a single lobe or nose piercing. Cartilage piercings such as helix, rook, daith and tragus are £35. More complex piercings range from £35 to £60. All prices include implant grade titanium jewellery.

Do you use a needle or a gun for piercings?

We always use a needle — never a piercing gun. Needle piercing is significantly safer, more precise, and leads to faster, healthier healing than gun piercing. It also causes less trauma to the tissue.

What jewellery do you use for new piercings?

All new piercings are fitted with implant grade titanium jewellery — the safest and most biocompatible material for healing piercings. Jewellery upgrades are available from £10. We do not pierce with steel, acrylic, or mystery metal.

Can you change jewellery in an existing piercing?

Yes — we're happy to change existing jewellery. We can only use jewellery from our own stock, and a charge applies for jewellery changes. Please contact us to arrange a jewellery change appointment.

How do I care for my new piercing?

We provide full aftercare instructions with every piercing. As a general guide, clean new piercings twice daily with sterile saline solution, avoid touching with unwashed hands, and don't rotate or move the jewellery. Follow-up advice is always available — just call us on 01905 799055.
