Dermal Filler FAQs

How much does dermal filler cost in Droitwich?

Dermal filler at Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich Spa starts from £75 for tear through treatment. Lip, cheek, chin, jawline and marionette filler start from £150 per area. All treatments include a full consultation with our qualified practitioners.

Is a consultation required before dermal filler?

Yes — a thorough consultation is always required before any aesthetics treatment. Our practitioners will assess your suitability, discuss your goals, review your medical history, and recommend the most appropriate treatment plan. To arrange a consultation, call us on 01905 799055.

How long does dermal filler last?

Results typically last 6 to 18 months depending on the area treated, the product used, and your individual metabolism. Lip filler generally lasts 6 to 12 months. Cheek, chin and jawline filler can last up to 18 months. Your practitioner will advise on expected longevity at your consultation.

Does dermal filler hurt?

Most clients experience only mild discomfort during treatment. We use products that contain lidocaine — a local anaesthetic — which helps minimise any discomfort during the procedure. Any redness or minor swelling typically settles within a few days.

What areas can be treated with dermal filler?

At Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich, we offer dermal filler for lips, cheeks, chin, jawline, marionette lines, and tear trough (under-eye area). Our practitioners will recommend the most suitable treatment for your individual concerns at consultation.
