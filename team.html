Meet the Team

The talented people behind your transformation

Our Expert Team

With a combined experience of over 60 years in the beauty industry, our talented team brings passion, creativity, and expertise to every appointment. From precision cuts to advanced aesthetics and body piercing, we're committed to delivering exceptional results and making you feel absolutely gorgeous.

Kylie

Owner & Senior Stylist

With over 18 years of experience, Kylie founded Gorgeous Hair Design in 2006 with a vision to create a sanctuary for hair care excellence. She is also qualified in hair piercings and aesthetics, and her passion for helping clients look and feel their best through professional treatments and genuine care truly defines the salon's ethos.

Annie

Senior Stylist
