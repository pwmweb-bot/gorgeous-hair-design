Lip Filler FAQs

How much is lip filler in Droitwich?

Lip filler pricing at Gorgeous in Droitwich Spa is confirmed at your consultation, as it depends on the amount of product and the result you'd like. We'll always give you a clear, honest quote before any treatment goes ahead.

Will my lips look natural?

Yes. Our approach is subtle and tailored — we enhance your natural lip shape rather than overfill. Whether you want a little more volume or better definition, we work with you for a balanced, natural-looking result.

What is lip filler made of?

We use hyaluronic acid dermal fillers — a substance naturally found in the body. As well as adding volume and definition, hyaluronic acid attracts moisture, helping your lips look hydrated and healthy.

How long does lip filler last?

Lip filler typically lasts between 6 and 12 months, depending on the product and how your body metabolises it. Many clients return for a top-up once or twice a year to maintain their look.

Does it hurt?

Most clients find it very manageable. Our fillers contain a numbing agent, and we can apply additional numbing cream for comfort. There may be mild swelling or tenderness afterwards, which settles within a few days.
