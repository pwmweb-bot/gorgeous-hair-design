Haircut FAQs

How much is a haircut in Droitwich?

Haircuts at Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich Spa start from £30, including a consultation, cut and blow dry. The exact price depends on your hair length and the stylist you book — we'll always confirm your price at the consultation before we begin.

Do you cut mens and childrens hair?

Yes — we're a friendly unisex salon welcoming ladies, men and children. From sharp men's cuts to a child's very first haircut, our Droitwich team will make everyone feel at ease.

Do I need to book, or can I walk in?

We recommend booking ahead, as we're often fully booked — especially on Fridays and Saturdays. You can book online any time, or call us on 01905 799055. If you're hoping for a walk-in, give us a ring and we'll let you know what's available.

What happens during the consultation?

Every cut starts with a proper chat. We look at your face shape, hair texture and lifestyle, and talk through the look you want — so your finished cut suits you and is easy to manage at home, not just in the salon.

How often should I get my hair cut?

For most styles, a trim every 6 to 8 weeks keeps your hair healthy and your shape looking sharp. Shorter styles may need refreshing sooner, while longer hair can often go a little longer. Your stylist will advise what's best for your hair.
