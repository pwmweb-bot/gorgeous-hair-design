Hair Colour FAQs

How much does hair colour cost in Droitwich?

At Gorgeous Hair Design in Droitwich Spa, full head colour starts from £50. Half head highlights start from £70 and full head highlights from £80. Toner is £32. Prices vary depending on hair length and the result you're looking to achieve.

How long does a hair colour appointment take?

A full head colour typically takes around 1.5 to 2 hours including application and a blow dry. Highlight appointments tend to take 2 to 3 hours depending on the technique and how much hair is being coloured.

Do I need a consultation before my first colour appointment?

We always carry out a full colour consultation before any colour service. This allows us to assess your hair condition, understand your desired result, and recommend the best technique and products for you. If you've had any DIY colour or chemical services recently, please let us know when booking.

What's the difference between highlights and balayage?

Highlights use foil to section and lighten specific pieces of hair, creating a more uniform, traditional lightened effect. Balayage is freehand-painted for a softer, more natural gradient. Highlights need more frequent maintenance, while balayage grows out more gracefully. Our stylists can advise on the best option for your hair and lifestyle.

How often should I get my hair coloured?

Most single-process colour clients return every 4 to 8 weeks to cover roots and keep colour vibrant. Highlights typically last 8 to 12 weeks before needing a refresh. We'll give you personalised aftercare advice to help your colour last as long as possible.
